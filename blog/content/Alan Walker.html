Alan Walker Top 5 Song

Best Top 5 music from Alan Walker ,September 1, 2020

Alan Walker (Alan Olav Walker) - English-Norweygian music producer


Top 5 :Diamond Heart

Diamond Heart is a song made by Alan Walker, Thomas Troelsen, Kristoffer Haugen, Edvard Normann, Anders Frøen, Gunnar Pettersen, Yann Bargain, Victor Verpillat, Fredrik Olsen, and Sophia Somajo, and is sung by Sophia Somajo. It was released on the 28th of September, 2018, and was later made a song in the album Different World. Its music video also belongs in a story trilogy. The other songs in the trilogy are Tired, Darkside, and All Falls Down.


Top 4 :Darkside

Darkside is a song made by Alan Walker, Jamie Stenzel, Lars Rosness, Markus Arnbekk, Fredrik Olsen, Anders Frøen, Gunnar Pettersen, James Njie, Jesper Borgen, Tommy Verdi, Atle Pettersen, William Larsen, and Andrew Frampton, and is sung by Tomine Harket and Au/Ra. It was released in 2018. Its music video also belongs in a story trilogy featuring 4 of Alan Walker's songs, with the other songs in the trilogy being Tired, All Falls Down, and Diamond Heart.


Top 3 :Sing Me To Sleep

Sing Me to Sleep is a song produced by Alan Walker, Jesper Borgen, Anders Frøen, Gunnar Pettersen, Thomas La Verdi, and Magnus Bertelsen, with vocals from Iselin Solheim. It was released on the 3rd of June, 2016, and was later made part of Walker's popular album Different World.


Top 2 :Alone

Alone is a song that was produced by Alan Walker and sung by Noonie Bao. It was released on the 2nd of December, 2016, and was later included in Walker's album Different World.


Top 1 :Faded

"Faded" is a hit song by Norwegian record producer and DJ Alan Walker. Incorporating vocals provided by Norwegian singer Iselin Solheim, the single was originally set to be released on 25 November 2015, but was delayed to 3 December 2015. The song was highly successful, peaking in the top 10 in most of the countries it charted in, and reached the top spot in more than 10 countries. It is currently the 18th most viewed video on YouTube, with over 2.5 billion views, 17 million likes and 473 thousand dislikes as of September 2019 as well as being the 28th most streamed song on Spotify, with over 1 billion streams as of September 2019.
